about Shotokan school

Shotokan Karate Youth Association (Regd. No : F-65392)

Recognized by Japan International Traditional Shotokan Karate Federation (OSAKA JAPAN) recognized by Karate Association of India (KAI). Recog.by : Fit India (ministry Of Youth Affairs And Sports) The SKYA is by far the largest karate organisation in India. The S.K.Y.A India Head Office employs professional staff and along with it dedicated instructors and senior members. Our association promotes a positive, professional and friendly environment, allowing students to develop themselves by being part of the most prestigious karate association in India : The SHOTOKAN KARATE YOUTH ASSOCIATION INDIA.


Any student or Karate Instructor wishing to join SKYA is Welcome. Any Instructor from any place in India is most Welcome for a single, group or state affiliation.

Shotokan Karate & Japanese Style

  • Shotokan Karate is a traditional Japanese martial art focused on strong, controlled techniques and discipline. It offers benefits like confidence, focus, and self-defense. People join for personal growth or to help their children
  • The club teaches Shotokan Karate in a friendly and positive environment, welcoming individuals and families to participate or observe classes.

Shotokan has specialized in martial arts since 1986 and has one of the most innovative programs in the nation.
