We train with traditional methods to develop and create an effective SKYA Shotokan Karate & yoga, emphasizing Speed, Strength, Power and a range of techniques for every part of one's body. Organizing and participating different National and International Seminar, Grand Master Training Camps, Championships, Tournaments, workshop.
